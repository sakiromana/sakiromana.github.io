Mood Light

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 2026

Mood Light is an ambient light and sound app developed by Sakiromana. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Mood Light does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Stored on Device

The app saves your preferences locally on your device (chosen colour preset, sound track, and effect settings) so they are restored the next time you open the app. This data never leaves your device.

Sharing

The app includes a share button that opens your device's native share sheet. Any sharing is initiated entirely by you and is handled by your device's operating system. Mood Light does not access, read, or store the content you share.

Third-Party Services

Mood Light does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Mood Light does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if advertising is added), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
