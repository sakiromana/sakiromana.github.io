Mood Light

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

Mood Light is a free, ad-supported ambient light and sound app developed by Sakiromana. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Mood Light does not require you to create an account. We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service, which may collect or process information in order to serve ads:

  • Google AdMob — We use AdMob to show app open ads when you return to the app after using another app. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, usage data, and advertising-related information to serve ads and measure performance. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Stored on Device

The app saves your preferences locally on your device (chosen colour preset, sound track, and effect settings) so they are restored the next time you open the app. This data never leaves your device.

Sharing

The app includes a share button that opens your device's native share sheet. Any sharing is initiated entirely by you and is handled by your device's operating system. Mood Light does not access, read, or store the content you share.

Children's Privacy

Mood Light is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads shown in the app are served by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's own policies and applicable regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
