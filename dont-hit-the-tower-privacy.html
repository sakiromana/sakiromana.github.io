Don't Hit the Tower - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Sakiromana ("we", "us", or "our") built Don't Hit the Tower as a free, ad-supported game. This page explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Don't Hit the Tower does not require you to create an account with us. We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address through the app.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, which may collect or process information in order to provide their features:

  • Google AdMob - We use AdMob to show app open ads and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, usage data, and advertising-related information to serve ads and measure performance. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Play Games Services - We use Google Play Games Services for optional sign-in and leaderboard functionality. If you sign in with Google Play Games, Google may process your player profile, leaderboard submissions, and related service data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores gameplay data locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Adventure progression, unlocked skins, and unlocked medals
  • Audio settings and other gameplay preferences

This local data is used to provide the game experience and can usually be removed by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Leaderboards

If you choose to use the leaderboard feature, your endless high score may be submitted to Google Play Games Services. This functionality is optional and depends on signing in with Google Play Games on your device.

Children's Privacy

Don't Hit the Tower is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads shown in the app are served by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's own policies and applicable regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
